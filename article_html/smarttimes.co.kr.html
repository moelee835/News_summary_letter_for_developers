Microsoft Integrates Anthropic AI Into Copilot to Expand Agent Capabilities

  • 기자명 Yeran Kim
  • 2026.03.10 18:37
  • Re 0
이 기사를 공유합니다

Microsoft is expanding its Copilot platform by adding artificial intelligence technology from startup Anthropic as demand grows for autonomous AI agents capable of handling complex tasks.

The company announced a new tool called Copilot Cowork, which is based on Anthropic’s Claude Cowork system. The software is designed to assist users with advanced tasks such as building applications, generating spreadsheets, and organizing large data sets with limited supervision.

Copilot Cowork will initially be available to early-access users later this month as Microsoft begins testing the feature. Some usage will be included in the company’s existing Microsoft 365 Copilot subscription, which costs US$30 per user per month, with additional usage offered separately.

The integration also introduces Anthropic’s latest Claude Sonnet models to Microsoft 365 Copilot. Previously, the service relied primarily on artificial intelligence models developed by OpenAI.
